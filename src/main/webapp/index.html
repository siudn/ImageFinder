Eulerity Take-Home Challenge: ImageFinder, an Image Scraping Web Crawler

The crawler will take longer for more complex sites (so please be patient after clicking the button). Testing websites with my personal runtimes are listed in text-links.txt.

To increase or decrease the level of depth you would like to crawl, increment or decrement the MAX_DEPTH variable in Crawler.java.

Images will be displayed after crawling. The full JSON array can also be viewed from the console (press F12 > console).

Logos or icons are specified with a caption under the image (not 100% successful).

Note: if duplicate images are displayed, the website has their logos/icons as img elements on every page.
