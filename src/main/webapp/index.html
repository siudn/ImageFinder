Eulerity Take-Home Challenge: Image Scraping Web Crawler

The crawler will take longer for more complex sites (so please be patient after clicking the button). Runtimes for specific websites are provided in text-links.txt.

The MAX_DEPTH variable can also be changed in Crawler.java to decrease/increase the number of pages you would like to crawl.

After crawling is done, the full JSON array can also be seen in the console (press F12 > console).

Logos or icons are also specified with a caption under the image (not 100% successful).

Note: if duplicate images are displayed, the website has their logos/icons as img elements on every page.
